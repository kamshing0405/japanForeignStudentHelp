"Emergency response" refers to taking swift and appropriate action in "no-wait" situations where life or property is in danger, such as <span class="font-bold bg-linear-to-t from-yellow-400/70 from-10% to-transparent to-50% dark:from-green-400/70">fires, natural disasters, accidents, sudden illness, or major business disruptions</span>.

#### <div class="custom-title">Emergency Phone Numbers (24 Hours, Free)</div>

First, make sure to memorize these three essential numbers.

| Number | Service                       | Usage & Notes                                                                                |
| ------ | ----------------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 110    | Police (Crimes/Accidents)     | Example: Use if you are a `victim` of a crime, `theft`, or involved in a `traffic accident`. |
| 119    | Fire/Ambulance (Fire/Illness) | Example:`fire` or an `ambulance`                                                             |
| #7119  | Emergency Consultation Center | Consult here if you are `unsure` whether to call an ambulance.                               |

#### <div class="custom-title">Preparing for Disasters (Earthquakes / Typhoons)</div>

Japan is a country prone to <span class="font-bold bg-linear-to-t from-yellow-400/70 from-10% to-transparent to-50% dark:from-green-400/70">earthquakes and typhoons</span>. Please prepare the following <span class="font-bold bg-linear-to-t from-yellow-400/70 from-10% to-transparent to-50% dark:from-green-400/70">three points</span>.

<div class="flex items-center text-center gap-2 h-6 mt-6">
    <span class="flex items-center justify-center w-6 h-6 bg-[#00873c] text-white rounded-full text-sm font-bold shrink-0">1</span>
    <p class="text-[#00873c] font-bold text-lg pl-0.5">Identify Evacuation Sites</p>
</div>

<br>

<img src='../../../images/hinansyo.jpg' class='rounded-2xl w-136'>

Check a hazard map to locate the nearest "<span class="font-bold bg-linear-to-t from-yellow-400/70 from-10% to-transparent to-50% dark:from-green-400/70">evacuation shelter</span>" (Hinansho) to your home or school.

<br>

<div class="flex items-center text-center gap-2 h-6 mt-6">
    <span class="flex items-center justify-center w-6 h-6 bg-[#00873c] text-white rounded-full text-sm font-bold shrink-0">2</span>
    <p class="text-[#00873c] font-bold text-lg pl-0.5">Emergency Survival Bag</p>
</div>

<br>

<img src='../../../images/bag.avif' class='rounded-2xl w-136'>

Keep a backpack ready with `water`, `food` (for 3 days), a `mobile battery`, a copy of your `Residence Card`, `cash`, and `emergency medicine`.

<br>

<div class="flex items-center text-center gap-2 h-6 mt-6">
    <span class="flex items-center justify-center w-6 h-6 bg-[#00873c] text-white rounded-full text-sm font-bold shrink-0">3</span>
    <p class="text-[#00873c] font-bold text-lg pl-0.5">Information Apps</p>
</div>

Beyond physical goods, it is vital to download disaster prevention apps to stay informed through <span class="font-bold bg-linear-to-t from-yellow-400/70 from-10% to-transparent to-50% dark:from-green-400/70">real-time updates</span>.

| Service Name                                                               | Features                                                                      |
| -------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| [Safety Tips](https://www.jnto.go.jp/safety-tips/eng/app.html)             | Supervised by JTA; sends multilingual earthquake alerts and evacuation orders |
| [NHK WORLD-JAPAN](https://www3.nhk.or.jp/nhkworld/en/multilingual_links/)  | Provides disaster news in English and other languages                         |
| [Yahoo! 防災速報](https://emg.yahoo.co.jp)                                 | Push notifications for earthquakes, typhoons, and heavy rain alerts           |
| [JMA（気象庁）アプリ](https://www.data.jma.go.jp/suishin/info/keitai.html) | Official source for earthquake, tsunami, and typhoon information              |

<br>

<div class="flex items-center text-center gap-2 h-6 mt-6">
    <span class="flex items-center justify-center w-6 h-6 bg-[#00873c] text-white rounded-full text-sm font-bold shrink-0">4</span>
    <p class="text-[#00873c] font-bold text-lg pl-0.5">Disaster Voice Message Service (171)</p>
</div>

This system lets you update <span class="font-bold bg-linear-to-t from-yellow-400/70 from-10% to-transparent to-50% dark:from-green-400/70">family and friends</span> on your safety when phone lines are congested during large-scale disasters.

- To Record: `171` → `1` → `Your Phone Number` → Record message
- To Play: `171` → `2` → `The Other Person's Phone Number` → Listen to message

<br>

#### <div class="custom-title">Illness at Night or on Holidays</div>

If you fall ill during the night or on a holiday, keep these <span class="font-bold bg-linear-to-t from-yellow-400/70 from-10% to-transparent to-50% dark:from-green-400/70">two</span> consultation numbers in mind.

| Number | Consultation Service             | Role                                                                                |
| ------ | -------------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| #7119  | Emergency Support Center         | Nurses provide advice on whether you need to see a doctor or how to handle symptoms |
| #8000  | Pediatric Emergency Consultation | Pediatricians and nurses provide advice on sudden illnesses in children             |
