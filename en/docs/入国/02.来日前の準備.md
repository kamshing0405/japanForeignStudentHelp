Once your visa is ready, the next step is pre-departure preparation to ensure a smooth start to life in Japan. Below is an important checklist based on the current situation

### <div class="relative font-bold text-2xl pb-3 after:absolute after:left-0 after:bottom-0 after:w-full after:border-b-4 after:border-dotted after:border-blue-600">Essential Preparations</div>

<span class="bg-blue-500 w-48 px-3 py-1 rounded-2xl text-4 text-sm text-white">Starting preparations three weeks before departure will give you peace of mind</span>

<div class="flex items-center text-center gap-2 h-6 mt-6">
    <span class="flex items-center justify-center w-6 h-6 bg-[#00873c] text-white rounded-full text-sm font-bold shrink-0">1</span>
    <p class="text-[#00873c] font-bold text-lg pl-0.5">Valuables and Important Documents</p>
</div>

These items should not be placed in checked luggage and must be kept with you at all times

<img src='../../../images/item.png' class='rounded-2xl w-136'>

- `Passport`と`Visa`

- `Certificate of Eligibility` : Required at immigration inspection upon `entry`

- `Letter of Acceptance`：May be requested as proof of student status

- `Application for Permission to Engage in Activity Other Than That Permitted` Complete this in advance to obtain part-time work permission at the airport

- `Cash`（Japanese Yen）：Bringing approximately `50,000–100,000` yen is recommended for `transportation` and `meals` immediately after arrival, as some initial expenses may require cash only

- `Identification photos`（several）：Needed for issuing a student ID and various `administrative procedures`

- `Overseas travel insurance`（if required）

- `International Driving Permit`（if required）

<br>

<div class="flex items-center text-center gap-2 h-6 mt-6">
    <span class="flex items-center justify-center w-6 h-6 bg-[#00873c] text-white rounded-full text-sm font-bold shrink-0">2</span>
    <p class="text-[#00873c] font-bold text-lg pl-0.5">Preparing Finances and Communication</p>
</div>

<br>

<img src='../../../images/item2.png' class='rounded-2xl w-136'>

- Backup payment methods: `credit cards` and international money `transfer services`

- Communication options: Reserve a `prepaid SIM card` or `eSIM` for use in Japan in advance

<br>

<div class="flex items-center text-center gap-2 h-6 mt-6">
    <span class="flex items-center justify-center w-6 h-6 bg-[#00873c] text-white rounded-full text-sm font-bold shrink-0">3</span>
    <p class="text-[#00873c] font-bold text-lg pl-0.5">Other Items</p>
</div>

<br>

<img src='../../../images/item3.png' class='rounded-2xl w-136'>

- `Clothing`: Bring clothes suitable for Japan’s seasons; warm clothing is especially important in winter.

- `Regular medications`: Familiar medicines such as cold remedies or digestive medicine.

- `Personal seal` (<ruby>印鑑<rp>(</rp><rt>いんかん</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>): While it is less commonly required today, an inkan may still be requested for opening a `bank account` or `signing an apartment contract`. You can have one made in Japan, but having one in advance can make procedures smoother.

- `PC` or `tablet`: Essential for university assignments and online classes.

- `Plug adapter`: Electrical outlets in Japan use `Type A plugs`. The voltage is 100V, so please check whether your devices are compatible.

- `Seasonings` or `snacks` from your home country: Bringing familiar items can be comforting during the initial adjustment period.

- `Chargers` and a `Power bank`: A portable charger is recommended to avoid situations where your device runs out of power and no charging location is available.

<br>

<div class="flex items-center text-center gap-2 h-6 mt-6">

    <p class="text-[#00873c] font-bold text-lg pl-0.5">Registering for Visit Japan Web</p>
</div>

A few days before departure, complete the following registrations on [Visit Japan Web](https://services.digital.go.jp/visit-japan-web/) and save the QR code as a screenshot.

When registering on（[Visit Japan Web](https://services.digital.go.jp/visit-japan-web/)）for immigration inspection, you will need to enter your `residential address` in Japan.

<div class=' flex justify-center'>

| Situation                                    | Action                                                                      |
| -------------------------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| If your dorm or apartment is already decided | Keep a record of the `address and phone number`                             |
| If not yet decided                           | Enter `the address of the hotel` where you will stay for the first few days |

</div>
